Payments are handled by Stripe. We do not store your full card number. Stripe may collect payment, billing, fraud-prevention, and tax-related information according to its own policies.

3. How we use information

We use information to review your request, deliver paid services, communicate with you, prepare project recommendations, maintain records, improve the site and services, and comply with legal or security requirements.

We may use service providers for hosting, checkout, email, project delivery, analytics, security, and document handling. These providers should only use information as needed to provide their services.

The site may use basic technical logs from hosting providers. If analytics tools are added later, they will be used to understand site visits and improve conversion. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Confidential project data

Project materials are used to perform the work. Do not submit passwords, regulated personal data, protected health information, financial account data, or other highly sensitive data unless we have agreed in writing that it is required.

7. Retention

We keep information as long as needed for service delivery, records, security, legal obligations, and reasonable business purposes. You can ask us to delete information when it is no longer needed.
